WHY A BLOG?

In the retrospect of having now built this site, as diminutive as it may be, I feel its necessary to explain why I think its a good idea.

For the most part, I have not been an active participant within the communities of the technologies and practises I am involved in. I lurk on notable blogs, RFC's, and threads as a passive observer with no notable presence. If I were asked to recount examples where I contributed in these spaces I would fail to recall any. And there is nothing inherently wrong about that! Yet it bothers me.
